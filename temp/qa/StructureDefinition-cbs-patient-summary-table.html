Title:Case Based Surveillance Patient Profile
Status:Experimental as of 2021-01-01
Definition:

Defines constraints and extensions to the patient resource in order to meet the needs of public health surveillance programs while providing as much alignment with US Core requirements as possible. Mapping between similar concepts implemented with different value sets (ie: race, ethnicity, or sex assigned at birth) may be required.
